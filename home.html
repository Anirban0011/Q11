INNOVATIVE ACADEMICS

Engineering courses right from First semester instead of 12th++ courses in First year Skill First, Theory Later, Projects Integrated model for sound foundation of fundamentals Fractal Academics with short modular courses to enable inclusion of latest technologies in course curriculum 'T'-Education model to enable students to acquire both breadth and depth of knowledge Course curriculum judiciously designed to offer balanced exposure to both Hardware and Software with diverse set of tools (such as Arduino, Rasberry Pi, Sensor, RTL-SDR, Python, C, MATLAB, Cadence, Oracle etc.) for hands-on experience Fosters project-based learning with FOUR mini projects, one each in 4th, 5th, 6th and 7th semester and ONE major project in 8th semester.

RESEARCH & INNOVATION

Encourages students by providing them financial support to participate in National/International Conferences, Seminars, Symposiums, Workshops to present their research work Encourages students to publish their research work in journals and conferences Provides financial support to students to prepare prototypes and for experimentation.A student can choose to do the same project from 5th to 8th semester for increased depth, or different projects for increased breadth Elective subjects and multidisciplinary project options encourage students to develop interdisciplinary skills Strong Industry-linkages for internships and industry-elective subjects in course curriculum.

OVERALL PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT

Nurtures a campus culture with high ethical standards Institute extra-curricular activities are student driven to help students develop leadership, team spirit, sense of responsibility, time management, financial management, communication skill, inter-personal skill and many other soft skills, which are necessary for professional success Student Activity Centre (SAC), consisting of various types of clubs, is completely managed by the students. SAC also conducts many extra-curricular activities. Focussed on soft skills development, specially designed Workshops, Social Events and Invited talks are frequently organized The institute helps students identify and pursue their PASSION.
