Academic Structure

Fractal Academics

The recent developments in the subject areas along with the innovation in Course structuring like ‘Fractal Academics’ and ‘Skill Orientation’ were incorporated in the Curriculum design in XYZ University. The innovative idea of ‘Fractal Academics’ though originated from our partner universities in the Americas, Europe and Asia is being implemented in some of the newer colleges around the world as well. XYZ has embedded issues like ‘Job-Orientation’ and ‘Research Enabling’ as the driving force behind the Curriculum development, while encouraging the entrepreneurial instinct in the students. The XYZ Chair Senate proposed a design of such curriculum which while making students Industry ready, also enables them to get into “Undergraduate Researching”. It is being endeavored to encourage the students in their Minor and Major project work to undertake research-oriented problems so that they may get exposed to many of the prevailing Science and Technology problems while supporting Faculty members in their Project/Research work as well.
