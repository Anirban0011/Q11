About Us

Graduate Programmes

XYZ University currently runs three undergraduate programs: B.Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) , B.Tech. in Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) and B.Tech. in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAI) . These credit-based programs are designed to foster core skills with innovation and entrepreneurship skills among students. The dynamic structure of the curriculum orchestrates with the changing needs of industry and academia. We follow the Fractal Curriculum.
XYZ offers bachelor level programme in following disciplines:

  • B.S./B.E./B.Tech
    • Computer Science & Enginering
    • Data Science & Artificial Intelligence
    • Electronics & Communication Engineering

Postgraduate programmes

The master’s degree generally requires a minimum of one academic year of study. Admission to XYZ for the master’s degree does not necessarily imply an automatic commitment by XYZ beyond that level of study. This program provides a higher level of professional competence than is required by the program leading to the master’s degree, but less emphasis is placed on creative research than in the doctoral program. XYZ currently provides post graduate courses in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) and Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE).
XYZ offers bachelor level programme in following disciplines:

  • M.S./M.E./M.Tech
    • Computer Science & Enginering
    • Electronics & Communication Engineering
