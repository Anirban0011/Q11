Department Of Data Science & Artificial Intelligence

About Us


Data science is transforming business, industry, government, social interaction and research. Our Department of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAI) is at the vanguard of research into the technologies that underpin this revolution.We're unlocking the potential of large data sets across a wide range of applications. Our work is facilitating better health outcomes for elderly members of the community and helping businesses make more informed decisions for the future. Our cross-disciplinary approach to problem-solving means we work collaboratively with some of the world’s leading data scientists and external partners, ensuring we have the capabilities to deliver answers. Data science is an emerging interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from both structured and unstructured data. The discipline of artificial intelligence involves integrating knowledge into programs that can handle data and solve complex problems. In today’s context, expertise in the domains of data science and artificial intelligence is in great demand. A lot of opportunities exist in these emerging domains. Keeping in view these latest trends, XYZ University has taken active steps to start the Department of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence so that the same can become a vibrant center of activities in these domains. The school aims to contribute to shaping a pool of highly qualified professionals in this emerging field by aligning its activities in the direction of national-level initiatives through its undergraduate and postgraduate programs.
